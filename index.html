Massage Services

Relaxing Massage

A soothing full-body experience designed to melt away stress and quiet the nervous system. Through slow, flowing strokes inspired by the rhythm of the ocean, this treatment promotes deep relaxation, improved sleep and a gentle return to balance.

Sports Massage

A focused and energizing treatment that targets tight muscles, overuse areas and limited mobility. Ideal for active clients, athletes and anyone experiencing tension from training or daily stress. Helps improve recovery, reduce soreness and restore natural movement.

Connective Tissue Massage

A therapeutic deep-tissue approach that works through the layers of fascia to release long-held tension. Gentle cupping and precise pressure help free restrictions, improve posture and create a sense of openness in the body. Perfect for chronic tightness, stiffness and limited mobility.

Ocean Release

Harmony Waves’ signature full-body flow inspired by the ocean’s movement. Combining relaxing and therapeutic techniques with a rejuvenating facial ritual using gua sha and jade stones, this treatment brings deep grounding, renewal and a radiant, lifted feeling.

Massagebehandlinger

Afslappende Massage

En blid og helhedsorienteret behandling, der sænker stressniveauet og beroliger nervesystemet. Med rolige, flydende strøg inspireret af havets rytme skaber massagen dyb afslapning, bedre søvn og en naturlig følelse af balance.

Sportsmassage

En målrettet og energigivende behandling, der løsner spændte muskler, overbelastede områder og nedsat bevægelighed. Perfekt til aktive mennesker, sportsudøvere og dem, der oplever spændinger fra træning eller arbejde. Fremmer restitution, mindsker ømhed og genskaber fri bevægelse.

Bindevævsmassage

En terapeutisk dybdegående behandling, der arbejder gennem lagene af bindevæv for at frigøre dybe spændinger. Blid cupping og præcist tryk hjælper med at løsne restriktioner, forbedre kropsholdning og skabe mere plads og lethed i kroppen. Ideel ved kroniske spændinger, stivhed og begrænset bevægelighed.

Ocean Release

SHarmony Waves’ signaturbehandling inspireret af havets rolige bevægelser. En helkropsoplevelse, der kombinerer afslappende og terapeutiske teknikker med en fornyende ansigtsmassage med gua sha og jadesten. Efterlader kroppen afspændt og sindet løftet med en naturlig glød.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of massage do you offer? ▼
At Harmony Waves, you can choose between Relaxing Massage, Sports Massage, Deep Tissue / Connective Tissue work, and our signature Ocean Release treatment. Each session is tailored to your body's condition and goals.
How do I know which massage is right for me? ▼
For stress and calming — choose Relaxing Massage.
For soreness, tightness and recovery — choose Sports or Deep Tissue.
For a full-body flow with facial rejuvenation — choose Ocean Release.
Where is your massage studio located? ▼
We are located at Godthåbsvej 109A, 2000 Frederiksberg, inside Beauty Bar MoonLight. Easy access by bus, bike and car.
Is there parking nearby? ▼
Yes, there is paid street parking on Godthåbsvej and the surrounding streets. Most clients find parking within 1–3 minutes walking distance.
How long does a session last? ▼
Most treatments are 60 or 90 minutes. 90-minute sessions allow deeper work and more relaxation.
Do you offer gift cards? ▼
Yes — gift cards are available for any occasion. You can contact me or order one via the booking link.
Can I book online? ▼
Absolutely — booking is available 24/7 through our Planway system.
What should I expect during my first visit? ▼
We will talk about your needs, tension areas, preferred pressure and any injuries. You will be guided throughout the session so you feel safe and relaxed.
Do you work with athletes or chronic tension? ▼
Yes — many clients come for sports recovery, posture-related tension, and back/neck tightness. Treatments are adapted individually.
Are there any contraindications? ▼
Massage may not be recommended if you have fever, acute inflammation, recent surgery or severe cardiovascular conditions. If unsure — feel free to ask.
